Project Description

The Rapid E-BusPass project is a real-time solution designed to simplify the bus pass registration and renewal process for students, effectively addressing the common frustrations associated with manual procedures at colleges.

With this system, students can easily register for a new bus pass or renew an existing one online, eliminating the need to wait in long lines. The system sends timely reminders via SMS or email as the pass's expiration date approaches, ensuring that students never miss their renewal deadline. Payment options are flexible, allowing students to use vouchers, credit cards, or other wire transfer methods for convenience.

To get started, students simply register by submitting required details, including a photo and address proof. Once their information is verified, their bus pass is approved. This online bus pass registration application will help students save time, streamline the process, and offer a more convenient solution to an otherwise tedious task.
